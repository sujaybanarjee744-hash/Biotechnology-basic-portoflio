About Me

03 — Work

Research Projects

PRJ — 001

Plant Tissue Culture Study

Investigated the principles of in vitro plant propagation using Murashige & Skoog (MS) medium. The study focused on callus induction, shoot organogenesis, and the influence of plant growth regulators (auxins and cytokinins) on tissue differentiation in model plant species.

Tools
MS Medium Laminar Flow Autoclave pH Meter
Outcome: Successfully induced callus formation and documented regeneration stages; gained hands-on proficiency in sterile technique and growth regulator optimisation.
PRJ — 002

Water Quality Analysis via Microbial Testing

Conducted microbiological assessment of water samples from three distinct local sources using standard coliform detection protocols. Employed MPN (Most Probable Number) method and membrane filtration technique to quantify bacterial contamination levels and assess potability.

Tools
MPN Method LTB Broth Membrane Filter Colony Counter
Outcome: Identified unsafe coliform levels in one source exceeding WHO limits; compiled a structured report with remediation recommendations aligned with BIS standards.
PRJ — 003

DNA Extraction & Visualisation Experiment

Performed isolation of genomic DNA from onion and banana samples using a detergent-salt lysis protocol. Followed by ethanol precipitation and gel electrophoresis to verify extraction quality and estimate molecular weight bands against a standard DNA ladder.

Tools
Agarose Gel Ethidium Bromide UV Transilluminator Centrifuge
Outcome: Achieved clear DNA band visualisation; understood the practical nuances of lysis efficiency, precipitation timing, and gel loading that are critical in molecular biology workflows.
